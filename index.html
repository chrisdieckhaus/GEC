THE IDEA IS SIMPLE:

Teach young girls how to save the world through STEM fields by actually letting them do it.

Girls Engineering Change seeks to close the 7:1 male to female gender gap in the Science, Technology, Engineering, & Math fields by encouraging girls through the construction of engineered products and increasing their confidence through a network of college mentors who help them throughout their education. Our curriculum enables middle school and high school girls to build a tangible product to be used in developing world nations, such as medical equipment testers, clean-burning cook stoves, and water filters. GEC has reached over 300 girls at 4 universities and is expanding annually.
